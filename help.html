Welcome to OS13k!

OS13k is a tiny pseudo operating system designed for a JS13k community project. It has native support for Shadertoys, Dweets, ZzFX, medals, and much more.

Your mission, if you choose to accept it, is to help us fill it up with as much cool stuff as we can fit.

Please keep this project confidential!
